How to Sell With Instagram Reels and Stories

用短视频讲故事

多平台注意短视频有好处，可以让视频更容易定位。One of the advantages of having so many platforms focusing on short videos at the same time is that it’s easier to repurpose our videos.

过去，品牌营销发布内容，需要符合每个平台的特性，不同图形，正文，标题，排版等等，这会花费很多时间。而现在每个平台的要求差不多，这意味着，一旦创建一个视频，可以出现在很多平台上。In the past, brands and marketers have had to be strategic about making content appear more native to each platform when they wanted to cross-post. This involved creating different-sized graphics, sometimes rewording copy or captions, and spending time on each post. Every platform’s stories are all at the same size, meaning that once you create your video, it’ll appear native to any platform.

short video 更具有人情味，让品牌比专门制作的典型视频更平易近人，这更接近与一种对话的形式，让人更接近观众。Reels and Stories both offer a full-screen, immersive view. This view is much more personable and allows your brand to be more approachable than your typical produced video. Part of this is because the full-screen vertical video more closely represents a conversational view, one you might associate with a FaceTime call or video chat. It brings you closer to your audience.

And finally, short videos are easier to make than longer, more produced videos. They require less time and a lot of times businesses don’t even have to try to think of something original or innovative to say. In fact, with a lot of short videos, the aim is to hop on a trend. Look for a popular trend and then adapt it to your feed. You don’t have to create something completely unique or original; and in fact, it can even be a benefit not to. 短视频比长视频制作容易，，需要时间更少，不需要试图去原创新的东西。短视频就是顺势而为，寻找流行趋势，然后根据自己的要求进行调整，不必创造完全独特的东西。事实上，不去创造甚至是一种好处。

法一：通过INS Stories 与INS Reels将关注变为追随者 INS故事和INS视频差不多，通常是动画或录制的视频，包括贴纸、文本和音乐。但其两者有区别。

展示方式不同两者。Instagram reels are discoverable through hashtags and the Explore feed. Instagram Stories, on the other hand, are typically shown only to your existing network. There’s no way to explore and discover stories the same way you can with Instagram reels.

使用INS Reels张贴相关内容和使用相关标签有助于在观众面前展示展示自己。This means that Instagram Reels is what will grow your audience. Posting relevant content and using relevant hashtags will help you get in front of your audience.

INS Reels增加受众，张贴相关标签。通过INS Reels吸引，然后使用INS Stories故事培养观众。Then once your reels draw them in, Instagram Stories is the ideal spot to nurture your audience.

使用两者协同工作，INS Reels 应该用于引起好奇心，让人们关注你的个人资料，您还可以在标题中添加行动号召，鼓励人们去观看您的故事以获取更多信息或对其他的Reels进行更深入的了解。To get these two working together, your Instagram reels should create curiosity and get people over to your profile. You can also add a call to action (CTA) to your caption encouraging people to go watch your story for more information or an extended take on your reel.

Once people are following you, you can keep your Instagram story top of mind by posting regularly and frequently. The more often you can post in a day, the better. 一旦人们开始关注你，你就可以通过定期且频繁地将脑中大事发布到你的INS Stories，你在一天内容发帖的次数越多越好。

在进行short video时是否使用新的INS Account？ If you’ve already got a good following for your brand or business, and your plan is to start narrowing your content down into a niche to better serve your audience and grow, your audience will start to filter itself out. The people who followed you for those posts that weren’t relevant to your business will simply unfollow you. And at that point, because they were never really interested in your business anyway, you won’t have lost anything except a vanity metric. 如果你的品牌有了很好的追随者，你的计划是开始将你的内容缩小到一个利基市场，比便于更好地服务于你的受众并实现增长，你的受众将开始转向过滤本身。那些关注你的人会因为业务无关会取消关注你，因为他们无论如何都不会对你的业务真正感兴趣，所以你不会失去任何东西，除了一个虚荣心。

同时，你提供的解决方案和通过你的利基内容提供的价值将吸引更多的追随者，他们正在寻找你提供的解决方案，并有可能更接近于购买。Meanwhile, the solutions you give and the value you offer through your niche content will attract more followers who are looking for the solutions you’re offering and are potentially closer to buying.

法二：创建INS Reels 来吸引目标观众

The natural order of things on Instagram is that someone will be on their Explore feed or home feed and see a piece of content that catches their eye. Right now, more than likely, that content is going to be a reel. If they interact with that content and find value in it, they’ll tap through to learn more about the person who posted it. This is how people are drawn to your Instagram profile. INS之前的方式，有人会在他们的Explore Feed 或Home Feed上看到一条吸引他们眼球的内容。现在更有可能的的是，这些内容会成为一个Reels。他们与这些内容互动，并从中发现他们的价值，他们就会通过点击来了解更多关于发帖人的信息。这就是人们被 INS 个人资料所吸引的方式。

This also is where consistency is going to be very important. 这也是一致性非常重要的地方

The content on your feed should all relate back to your business or niche. There are some exceptions, especially for larger accounts. But for smaller accounts, it’s important to build your audience with your niche first, and then once you have a large audience, you can start branching out.订阅源上的内容应该都与您的业务或里利基市场相关。大账户例外，但对于小账户来说，重要的是首先利用你的利基市场来建立受众，然后一旦拥有了大量的受众，你就可以开始拓展业务了。

This is very important, going back to the idea that people are more discerning over who they follow. If you put out a reel about an at-home workout, and someone follows that video back to your profile and checks out the rest of your content, they’re probably not going to follow you if that was your one and only video about an at-home workout—especially if they’re interested in at-home workouts. 这一点非常重要，回到人们对追随谁更有鉴赏力的想法上来。如果你发布了一段关于在家锻炼的视频，然后有人跟着这条视频回到你的个人资料，查看了你的睡觉内容，如果这是你唯一一段在家锻炼的视频，他们很可能不会关注你–特别是如果他们对在家锻炼感兴趣的话。

If, on the other hand, you post a video about an at-home workout and someone taps on that video back to your profile where they see several more videos centered around at-home workouts, tips for working out at home, and related messaging, they’re much more likely to follow you. And not only that, but they’re much more likely to go back to your page over and over again. 另一方面，如果你发布了一条关于在家锻炼的视频，有人点击回到你的个人资料，他们看到了更多围绕在家锻炼的视频与小贴士，他们更有可能关注你，不仅如此，他们更有可能一遍又一遍的回到你的页面。

It’s also important that your bio accurately describes what someone is going to get out of following you—who you serve and how you serve them. Even if your content is all on-brand, people still need to see what they’re going to get out of following you.同样的，你的简介准确的描述了某人将从你的跟随中获得什么，你服务与谁，你是如何服务他们的。即使你的内容都是品牌上的，人们仍热需要看看他们会从关注你中得到什么。

调整趋势来适应市场Adapt Trends to Your Niche

不同的平台，Trends是领头羊，INS 和 TIK TOK 上正在发生的事情，你可以在其流行之前就在那里流行起来。When it comes to Instagram Reels or even TikTok, trends tend to lead the way. In fact, if you keep an eye on what’s happening on TikTok, you can sometimes hit a trend on Instagram before it starts to take off there.

However, you want to avoid just hopping on every trend. Rather than finding a trend and asking whether you can use that trend, you should be asking whether you can relate that trend back to your own niche and put your own spin on it. This will still allow you to catch attention from a trend but you don’t risk attracting the wrong people to your Instagram. 避免追随每一种趋势。应该问自己是否适合或利用这种趋势与自己的利基市场联系起来，并给它加上自己的旋律。这仍然可以让自己从这种趋势中受到关注，但你也不愿意让非目标用户关注你的INS。

For example, if you find a dance that’s trending and you decide to hop on the trend, if that dance video goes viral for you, it could attract a lot of new followers to your Instagram account. However, if that dance video doesn’t relate back to your niche, industry, or business in any way, then all of those new followers will quickly lose interest in the rest of your content. They were only there for that one trend video. 例如，网上正在流行一种舞蹈，你顺势而为，然后那段舞蹈在网上疯传，他可能会吸引很多新用户到你的INS上，但，如果舞蹈视频与你的利基、行业或业务没有任何关联，那么这些新的追随者很会就会失去兴趣，他们只是在哪里看到了一段流行的视频。

A few years ago, as people were finding others to follow on Instagram, those sorts of behind-the-scenes looks at their lives were important. We wanted to see a piece of someone’s world, their view, we were interested in their day, the food they ate. That’s not the case anymore. People have less time to spend on social media, and what’s more, they want to spend less time on social media.几年前，当人们在Instagram上寻找其他人关注时，那些关于他们生活的幕后观察是很重要的。我们想看看某人的世界，他们的观点，我们对他们的一天，他们吃的食物感兴趣。现在不是这样了。人们在社交媒体上花费的时间更少，更重要的是，他们希望在社交媒体上花费更少的时间。

They’re becoming more discerning over who they follow, which means they’re following brands that serve their purpose. They’re no longer going to follow just anybody. They’re going to curate their own Explore feed by choosing to follow particular people and brands.他们对追随谁变得越来越挑剔，这意味着他们追随的是服务于他们目的的品牌。他们再也不会追随任何人了。他们将通过选择关注特定的人和品牌来策划他们自己的探索馈送。

因此，您每次都应该创建与您的业务和利基市场相关的Instagram Reels内容。如果您需要创建原创内容来做到这一点，那是很棒的。如果你不能或没有时间看全是原创的内容，那就改编一种趋势，以一种新的方式来传递同样的信息，以帮助扩大你的受众。Therefore, you should be creating Instagram Reels content that relates back to your business and niche every time. If you need to create original content to do this, that’s great. If you can’t or don’t have time for all-original content, then adapt a trend as a new way to deliver the same message to help grow your audience.

法三：用INS Stories培养观众

So as we said, you’re going to use Instagram Reels as your introduction to a new audience. In most cases, people who’ve never heard of you before will find you through your Instagram reel. Given the right content and caption, you’ll use your reel to invite these people to view your Instagram story. 新的观众通过你的INS Reels发现你，通过正确的标题与内容，使用Reel邀请这些人查看您的INS Stories。

But that doesn’t mean you have to create new ideas or even new stories every time. You can curate stories that you think your audience will find valuable by sharing someone else’s story into your story. 但这并不意味着你每次都需要创造新的想法，甚至是新的故事。你可以通过别人的故事分享到你的故事中，来策划你认为你的观众会觉得有价值的故事。

You want to post valuable content—the kind that your audience won’t want to miss out on. People tend to check into Instagram several times a day, so by creating content they don’t want to miss out on, you increase the likelihood they’ll check out your stories every time they log in to check Instagram. 你想要发布有价值的内容–你的观众不想错过的那种内容。人们倾向于一天几次登录INS，所以通过创建他们不想错过的内容，你增加他们每次登录INS查看你的故事的可能性。

And because Stories is where you’re nurturing that relationship with your audience, that’s where you’ve earned the right to sell to them from, and therefore that’s where you’re going to get your sales from. 因为故事是你培养与观众关系的地方，那是你赢得向他们销售的权利的地方，因此你将从哪里获得你的销售。

Inside your Instagram stories, you can start posting interactive content that’ll help filter and qualify your audience.在你的Instagram故事中，你可以开始发布互动内容，这将有助于过滤并吸引你的观众。

For example, you can post a poll in which your audience can self-identify with a struggle you’re offering a solution to. The people who would answer no to that struggle might skip through the rest of your story or go back to their feed, and the people who say yes will tap into the next story to see what else you have to say about it.例如，你可以发布一个民意调查，让你的听众可以自我认同你正在提供解决方案的斗争。那些对这场斗争回答不的人可能会跳过你故事的睡觉，或者回到他们的帖子，而那些说是的人会进入下一个故事，看看你还有什么要说的。

And it doesn’t have to be a poll, it can also be a fun fact or a question sticker in which you ask people a question related to your niche and then later post the answers to that question along with your responses.它不一定是民意测验，也可以是有趣的事实，也可以是问题贴纸，你可以问别人一个与你的利基市场相关的问题，然后把问题的答案和你的回答一起发布出来。

这也是过滤未来通信的一个很好的方式，因为你可以回顾这些答案，向感兴趣的人发送链接和更多信息。This is also a great way to filter for a future correspondence, as you can go back through those answers to send links and more information to those interested.
